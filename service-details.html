Services

In accordance with our mission to promote women rights, peace and security, WWB provides services such as: consultancy/counseling, legal assistance, trainings and workshops.

  • Consultancy/Counseling services.

    • WWB provides customized service support including group level counseling as well as personal one to one guidance. Our counseling services cover a plethora of domains, ranging from; legal, mental health, career, educational and financial counseling.

    If you or your organization wish to make use of our consultancy services, please contact our office for more information.

  • Legal assistance

    • WWB is aware of the multitude of problems faced by the community regarding engagement in legal subjects. Timely advice on legal documents, low cost assistance, discounted rates for referral are provided to clients in order to ensure that they have all the help they need. By doing so, we provide the peace of mind that comes with having access to experts from the start to the finish.

  • Education and Training

    • WWB offers workshops and training courses that help people to know more about fundamental issues in the society. These courses are hugely focused on human rights awareness, peace, security, understanding of Sustainable Development issues, Gender equality, financial literacy and mental health awareness just to name a few.

    Workshop programs include: women empowerment workshops, Next Generation programmes and Conferences on Gender based violence.

  • Information Service.

    • Information is a lifesaving tool in every society. Due to the widespread ignorance and violation of human rights in Cameroon, WWB holds an open-house Information Centre and provides free information to the public. We provide a wide range of reference materials on international laws, human rights law, policy, peace and conflict resolution, and legal assistance. These materials are accessible to students, legal officers, researchers, and all those interested in gaining knowledge in the above-mentioned fields.

    The Information Centre is open on Monday to Friday from 9 am to 5 pm.

    The WWB Information Centre is continuously seeking to expand its existing resources. Therefore, donations of materials that would complement the materials are highly appreciated.
